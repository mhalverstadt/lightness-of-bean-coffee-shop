Who We Are

We are a farm to cup coffee roastery and shop. We work directly with farmers all over the world to purchase the highest quality beans at a fair price. We specialize in light to medium roasts in order to preserve the complexity and character of the coffee.

Next

Roastery

Every batch of beans are different. We use our 20+ years of experience to skillfully roast each batch in a way that brings out the best expression of each bean.

Next

Classes

Want to sharpen your coffee knowledge? Every Saturday and Sunday at 11am we have small classes on cupping, brewing methods, espresso techniques and more. Please sign up in person at the shop. Space is limited.

Next

Come Say Hi.

At The Unbearable Lightness of Bean, we try to make community and people central to what we do. Denver has been our home for over 20 years and we take pride in our involvement with local organizations and events around town.

Where To Find Us.

Please contact us if you have any questions about who we are, what we do, or if you just want a quality cup of coffee.
